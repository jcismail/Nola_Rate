# Content Compliance Snippets (LA/MS Expansion)

Use these snippets when writing or updating website/social advertising content for Texas Rate's Louisiana and Mississippi expansion pages.

## 1) Core Mortgage Disclaimer (Website/Footer)

```txt
This licensee is performing acts for which a mortgage Company License is required. C2 Financial Corporation is licensed by the Texas Department of Savings and Mortgage Lending; NMLS #135622. Loan approval is not guaranteed and is subject to lender review of information. All loan approvals are conditional and all conditions must be met by borrower. Loan is only approved when lender has issued approval in writing and is subject to lender conditions. Specified rates may not be available for all borrowers. Rate subject to change with market conditions. C2 Financial Corporation is an Equal Opportunity Mortgage Broker/Lender.
```

## 2) State Availability Language (LA/MS Expansion)

```txt
The services referred to herein are not available to persons located outside the state of Louisiana and Mississippi.
```

## 3) NMLS Consumer Access

```txt
NMLS Consumer Access: https://www.nmlsconsumeraccess.org
```

## 4) FHA/VA Reference Disclaimer

Use when FHA/VA are mentioned anywhere in page copy.

```txt
As a broker, C2 Financial Corporation has the ability to broker VA loans based on relationships with VA approved lenders. C2 Financial Corporation is not acting on behalf of or at the direction of HUD/FHA or the VA.
```

## 5) Safe Qualification Language

Preferred phrases:

```txt
Initial qualification
Eligibility review
Qualification depends on full borrower and property review
Program availability varies by lender guidelines
```

Avoid:

```txt
Guaranteed approval
Guaranteed pre-approval
Instant approval
No conditions
```

## 6) Safe Rate Language

If referencing rates, include APR clearly and nearby.

```txt
Example only: 6.50% interest rate (6.89% APR). Not all borrowers will qualify. Subject to lender approval and market changes.
```

Avoid:

```txt
Lowest rates available
Best rate in town
Guaranteed lowest rate
No-cost loan
```

## 7) Safe Down Payment / Payment Language

Preferred:

```txt
Down payment assistance may be available for qualified borrowers.
Program eligibility varies by lender, location, and borrower profile.
```

If using specific amounts, ensure full required disclosures are included (APR, repayment terms, etc.).

## 8) Testimonials Policy Snippet

```txt
Testimonials must be from actual borrowers and cannot be AI-generated. Remove or edit any testimonial that makes unsubstantiated superlative claims (for example, "best rates", "most knowledgeable", "lowest rate guaranteed").
```

## 9) Social/Online Profile Minimums

Include on any online business profile (website/social/industry listing):

```txt
Full company name: C2 Financial Corporation
MLO and company license identifiers
NMLS Consumer Access link
```
